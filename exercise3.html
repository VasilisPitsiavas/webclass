Tom Knight and the Lisp Machine

A novice was trying to fix a broken Lisp machine by turning
the power off and on.
Knight, seeing what the student was doing, spoke sternly: "You cannot fix a machine by just power-cycling it
with no understanding of what is going wrong." Knight turned the machine off and on. The machine worked.
